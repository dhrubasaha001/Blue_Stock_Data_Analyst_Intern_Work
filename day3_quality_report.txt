## KEY INSIGHTS

1. Mutual fund NAVs showed a strong long-term upward trend.

2. SBI Mutual Fund maintained the largest Assets Under Management.

3. Monthly SIP inflows increased steadily throughout the observed period.

4. Equity-oriented funds attracted the highest inflows.

5. Middle-aged investors represented the largest investing group.

6. T30 cities contributed more investment volume than B30 cities.

7. Industry folio counts nearly doubled over the study period.

8. Large-cap funds exhibited stronger positive return correlations.

9. Financial Services remained the largest portfolio sector.

10. Overall industry indicators reflected sustained growth in the Indian mutual fund ecosystem.
